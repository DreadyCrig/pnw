Welcome to the British American Business Council-Pacific Northwest (BABC-PNW). We are pleased to confirm your Corporate membership and thank you for supporting our mission to advance UK-US economic, business, trade and cultural connections.

Get started with your member benefits!

Established in 1995, BABC-PNW has long been the regional heartbeat of business and culture spanning both sides of the pond. This region has a long Anglo-American history, beginning as far back as the 1790s with exploration by British Royal Navy officer George Vancouver.

Today UK companies employ almost 28K Pacific Northwesterners across the region - spanning Alaska, Idaho, Oregon and Washington. And there is potential for more. An independent study, co-commissioned by UKinUSA, found that the Transatlantic Trade and Investment Partnership (TTIP), currently under negotiation, could create more than 30K additional jobs in our region. Beyond all this we share a common understanding of cloudy weather and that “drizzle” is not really rain!

Regionally, our chapter supports local businesses, as well as cultural links between both countries. We organize events throughout the year in a variety of key industries and feature opportunities for casual networking spanning from golf days to our wonderful annual Christmas luncheon.

The Chapter is, however, only as successful as its members, thus we encourage you to take part in our activities, and promote to your friends and colleagues. If you have ideas that relate to your firm or interests feel free to suggest them.

Should you have any questions about your membership or related benefits, please don’t hesitate to contact us at membership@babcpnw.org. We look forward to hopefully seeing you at our Annual General Meeting on March 18 and appreciate your membership commitment with us!
